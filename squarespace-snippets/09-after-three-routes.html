Design the system.

Some fixes cut across website structure, service positioning, content evidence, schema, measurement, internal workflows or agency ownership. The follow-on is a Systems Design Sprint: a focused engagement to turn the highest-priority recommendations into a joined-up plan, working briefs, and testable changes.

Typical owner Systems Design Sprint
ROUTE_03

Defer & monitor

Not every issue deserves immediate action. Some findings may be lower-value, lower-confidence, or less urgent. Those should be monitored rather than allowed to distract the team.

Typical owner You — with a watchlist
